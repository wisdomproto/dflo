권한별 제출 문구 (영문 — 복사해서 붙여넣기)

각 권한의 "How will you use this permission?" 칸에 그대로 붙여넣으세요. 핵심 메시지: "이 앱은 연세새봄의원이 자기 소유의 페이지·인스타·광고계정을 운영하는 내부 도구".

pages_show_list

pages_show_list — Our app is an internal business tool used by Yonsei Saebom Clinic's own staff. We use pages_show_list to display the list of Facebook Pages the business owns so the user can choose which Page to publish marketing content to and which Page's posts to promote as ads. Only the business's own Pages are accessed.

pages_read_engagement

pages_read_engagement — We read the business's own Page posts and their basic engagement to (a) show existing posts so staff can select one to boost as an ad, and (b) display post performance in our internal dashboard. Only the business's own Page data is read.

pages_manage_posts

pages_manage_posts — Our content studio publishes the clinic's own marketing content (photos, image carousels/albums, and videos/Reels) to the clinic's own Facebook Page on behalf of clinic staff. It is also used to publish a Page post that is then promoted as an ad.

pages_manage_metadata

pages_manage_metadata — Used to obtain the Page access token and read Page-level configuration required to publish content to, and read posts from, the clinic's own Page. We do not modify Page settings beyond what is required for publishing.

instagram_basic

instagram_basic — We read the clinic's own Instagram Business account (linked to its Facebook Page) to identify the account and its media, so that content can be published and existing posts can be selected for ad promotion. Only the business's own Instagram account is accessed.

instagram_content_publish

instagram_content_publish — Our content studio publishes the clinic's own marketing content (single image, carousel, and Reels) to the clinic's own Instagram Business account, scheduled or on demand by clinic staff.

ads_management

ads_management — Our app creates and manages advertising campaigns, ad sets, ads, custom audiences and lookalike audiences in the clinic's own ad account to promote the clinic's content. Campaigns are created in PAUSED state for human review before they are set live. All activity is on the business's own ad account.

ads_read

ads_read — Used to read campaign performance metrics (spend, impressions, clicks, reach, CTR, CPC) from the clinic's own ad account to display results in our internal reporting dashboard.

business_management

business_management — Used to access the business's own Business Portfolio assets (Pages, Instagram accounts, ad accounts) that the app needs to publish content and manage ads on behalf of the business. Only the owner business's assets are accessed.

스크린캐스트 (데모 영상) 시나리오

각 권한 제출 시 흐름을 보여주는 화면 녹화가 필요합니다. 한 번에 전체 흐름을 녹화하면 됩니다(내부 도구임을 설명하는 자막/음성 포함):

  1. dflo 마케팅 콘솔 로그인(내부 PIN) → "이 앱은 우리 병원 직원만 쓰는 내부 도구" 설명
  2. Meta 연결(OAuth) → 우리 소유 페이지/인스타/광고계정이 연결되는 화면 (pages_show_list·business_management·instagram_basic)
  3. 콘텐츠 스튜디오에서 우리 페이지/인스타에 게시물 발행 (pages_manage_posts·instagram_content_publish)
  4. 채널 피드에서 기존 게시물 불러오기 + 성과 보기 (pages_read_engagement·pages_manage_metadata)
  5. 광고 관리에서 캠페인 생성(PAUSED) + 성과 조회 (ads_management·ads_read)

자주 막히는 점

  • 비즈니스 인증 먼저 — 안 되어 있으면 ads_management 고급 액세스가 막힙니다.
  • 표준 vs 고급 액세스 — 표준은 앱에 역할 있는 사람(=우리)만, 고급은 일반까지. 우리 자산만 쓰면 표준으로도 많은 게 되지만, 자동 링크/전환 광고(object_story_spec)는 고급+라이브가 안전.
  • OAuth 리디렉션 URI 정확 일치 — 한 글자(끝 슬래시 포함)라도 다르면 거부.
  • 건강/의료 광고 정책 — 권한 카테고리와 별개로, 광고 문구는 효과 단정·개인 신체 특성 단정 금지(규정 박스 참고).
  • 심사 기간 — 비즈니스 인증·앱 검수는 수일~수주. 그동안 Ads Manager 수동 운영 + 픽셀 모수 적립을 병행.
